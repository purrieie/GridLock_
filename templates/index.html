Help

  1. Upload a single static-camera MP4 clip under Analysis.
  2. Draw a zone: click points around the area to monitor, then double-click (or press Close Zone) to seal it.
  3. Run Analysis. GridLock detects and tracks every vehicle, then flags any that cross out of your zone.
  4. Review the Overview charts, open the Reports tables, and watch the annotated videos — all downloadable.

The camera is assumed fixed, so a zone drawn on the first frame holds for the entire clip.
